Auctioneer's Notes

Please join us for an amazing two days at the Anoka County Fairgrounds. October 3rd has good quality collectibles including fishing lures and tackle boxes, spearing and duck decoys, old toys and advertising, beer items and signs, very nice antique furniture, glassware, carnival toys including tin games, oil cans and jars, table-top Victrola-type phonograph, Coleman stove and lamps, enamelware, collection of old canes, Land O'Lakes thermometer, old wagon, and deer antlers.

On Sunday, October 4th we will be selling items from an estate. The seller on Sunday loved buying items at Cabela's, Gander Mountain, and Northern Tool and Equipment and bought enough items to stock a small store. There are 100s of items and most are new in the box and never used. There will be 100s of camo articles and many lots of hunting and fishing accessories and ammunition.
