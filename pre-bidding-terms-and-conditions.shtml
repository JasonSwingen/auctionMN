Bidders must be at least 18 years old.

Before the start of the live auction online pre-bidding will be available. Pre-bidding on all online lots in this auction will close at 6 p.m. Central Daylight Time on Saturday, April 7th, 2018. The online pre-bids determine the live auction starting bids for those items. The live auction is expected to start at 7:15 pm on Saturday, April 7th, 2018. There is no online bidding during the live auction.

Online bidders may place a maximum bid. If so, AAA Auction will place bids for the online bidder by proxy up to the online bidder's maximum bid. The proxy bids will be increased only in the regular course to the amount necessary to win the item. Thus, it is possible that an online bidder may win an item for an amount less than his maximum bid. In case of a tie between an online bidder and a person who is present, the person present bidder will win.
