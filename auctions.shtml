Auctions
 
AAA Auction and Realty, LLC offers traditional auctions, consignment auctions, real estate auctions, and online auctions.
 
Traditional auctions can be conducted at our Auction Center in Anoka or at your location. If you need help moving your items to the Auction Center, we can help you with that. If we come to your location, we bring everything necessary to hold a good old-fashioned auction. We can also do all of the set-up or show you how to do it. If you don't have enough items for your own auction, we can add your items to other items we have for a consignment auction.
 
At this time we hold live traditional auctions and live consignment auctions when we have enough items for a good auction, so we suggest that you click on the e-mail notification link so we will be able to let you know when we are holding an auction.
 
Our experienced auctioneers are fully licensed to legally sell your cars, guns, and real estate.
