Our Home
The AAA Auction Center is at 7129 HIGHWAY 10 NW, RAMSEY MN 55303-6043 (Map). We're in the former Ramsey Wine & Spirits building at the intersection of Highway 10 and Ramsey Boulevard (Anoka County Road 56). We're across Highway 10 from a Holiday Stationstore, and across Ramsey Boulevard from a Burger King. Look for the bright red roof over our entryway.

Note: the Google Maps street view still shows an old gas station to the west of our place. It is no longer there.

From the east on Highway 10
Coming from the southeast on Highway 10, turn right into our driveway before you get to Ramsey Boulevard.

From the west on Highway 10
Coming from the west on Highway 10, turn north (left) onto Ramsey Boulevard. Exit Ramsey Boulevard directly across from Burger King's driveway, then pull around to the front of our building.

From the north on Ramsey Blvd.
Continue on Ramsey Boulevard past the Auction Center and cross Highway 10. Make a U-turn at the Holiday Station and go back north on Ramsey Boulevard. Exit Ramsey Boulevard directly across from Burger King's driveway, then pull around to the front of our building.
