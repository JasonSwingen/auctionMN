We Pay Referral Fees

Do you know anyone with something to sell? If you do and you refer them to us, we may be able to pay you a referral fee. If we sell a single item for $1,000 or more, we'll be happy to pay you a referral fee of $50 for every $1,000 in gross winning bid price on that one item. For example, if you refer someone to us with a car that we sell for $4,000, then when we are paid, we will pay you $200.
 
Also, if you refer an entire auction to us, then we will be happy to pay you a referral fee of $250. This only applies if the entire auction is made up of your referred items. It does not apply to our consignment auctions when we sell items belonging to several people.

All your friend has to do is mention your name as the referring person when he or she hires us.

Thank you to all of you who have been telling others about our business.
