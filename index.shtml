Location

Auctioneer's Notes

Are you tired of not being able to park in your garage? Have you thought it would be nice to upgrade your canoe or kayak this year? We've got just the thing for you.

Its easy. If you have a canoe or kayak to sell, just bring it to Midwest Mountaineering starting at 7 a.m. We'll help you unload it. We'll start the auction at 11 a.m. and pay you the same day. We charge a mere 15% and you can set a minimum sales price if you desire. You can also take in the Spring Expo at Midwest Mountaineering at the same time.
