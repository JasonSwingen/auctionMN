Auctioneer's Notes

Do you have a canoe, kayak or paddle board to sell? Do you need to upgrade to a newer model? There is no better time and no better place to sell or buy a new canoe, kayak or paddle board than the Midwest Mountaineering Spring Expo on April 29, at 11 am. Every year AAA Auction is honored to sell over 100 fine water crafts as part of the MWM Spring Expo. This is an old-fashioned, outdoor, live auction that draws large crowds and offers a wide selection of like new to used watercraft.

Its easy. If you have a canoe or kayak to sell, just bring it to Midwest Mountaineering starting at 7 a.m. We'll help you unload it. We'll start the auction at 11 a.m. and pay you the same day. You can also take in the Spring Expo at Midwest Mountaineering at the same time.
