New for 2018

The per item commission rate in 2018 is 25% for items selling up to $29. From $30 to $599.99 the commission is only 10%. For items selling for $600 or more, the Seller pays no commission at all. With limited exception, no bids will be accepted for less than $5.00. If no one bids $5.00, we will try to add to the items we are selling. If we are still unable to sell the group of items for at least $5.00, we will charge a $2 no-sale fee. We will again offer Sellers the opportunity to bring any items they want to the start of the auction at the “Seller’s Choice” tables. The difference there is that we will not add additional items to the lots we are selling, so if there is not at least a $5 bid on the single item or lot, the item or lot will not be sold and a no-sale fee will be applied.
