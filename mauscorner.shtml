General Information About Selling at the Auction

  1. Items can be dropped off and set up for the auction beginning at 6 a.m. on the morning of the auction.
  2. See the Auctioneer upon your arrival to sign a contract and be assigned a selling area. Seller’s items should be placed only in the area assigned.
  3. Large items may be dropped off earlier at Seller’s risk of loss only with prior approval from AAA Auction.
  4. There will be no commission charged for items selling for $1000 or more.
    There will be 10% commission charged for items that sell for $100 - $999.99. Sales over $2.50 and under $100 shall be charged 25% and any sale of $2.50 or less shall be charged a 50% commission.
  5. Sellers will set up their own items and may bring tables or leave their items on a trailer.
  6. AAA staff will not remove heavy items from trailers.
  7. For large value items, we recommend that Sellers write information on a card that the auctioneer can read to the bidders.
  8. Sellers will be paid on the day of the auction in most instances.
  9. Sellers shall clean up their areas and remove any unsold items or sold and abandoned items before receiving payment. They should ask a AAA member to remove their lot number stake after their area is cleaned up. They then should bring the stake to the office to receive payment.
  10. Large value items can be included in our auction ads if Seller provides AAA Auction with the information two weeks before the auction. Once advertised though, Seller shall follow through and offer the item for sale at the auction or pay a fee to AAA Auction.
  11. Reserves are allowed so long as the reserve is at least $300 and the Seller pays AAA Auction a No-Sale fee of $20 in advance which fee shall be refunded if the item sells.
  12. Sellers may bid on their own items, but if they win the item, they shall pay AAA auction above commission and 13% buyer’s premium.
  13. The Auctioneer will determine the order of sale; however, most often the selling order will be in the order of the arrival of the Sellers.
